Interior Design

What do we do?

Interior designers make the interior spaces aesthetically pleasing. safe, and functional. We do this by reading blueprints and determining the space for a selection of items such as furniture, lighting, and other materials. We also have to be aware of regulations for buildings, building codes, and accessibility standards. Engineers and architectures work closely with us as well.
