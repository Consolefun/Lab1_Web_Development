Fashion Design

What do we do?

Fashion designers create clothes that are functional as well as being aesthetically pleasing. We always consider the demographic that will wear our work and the situations in which our work will be worn.

Coursework/Skills

  • Fashion/Textile vocabulary and terminology
  • Consumer trends/behavior
  • Computer-aided design (CAD)
  • Hand drawing
  • Sewing/Tailoring
  • Fashion history and trends
  • Balancing proportions, colors, moods, and textiles
  • Human anatomy
  • Show promotion and production
